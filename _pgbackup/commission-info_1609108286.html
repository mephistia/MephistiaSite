Commission information

Want me to draw something for you? Please check my commission status on the site description, read below to check my prices and terms, and then contact me by any of my social media! You can find them in my About page or in the sidebar. I’ll gladly answer any questions or doubts!

Base prices

The base prices are set according to the visible portion of a single character’s body, with a simple background (a solid color, gradient, pattern or edited image). A bust or icon drawing is a (usually) square piece focused on the face of the character(s), cropped just below the shoulders or middle-chest. A half body drawing is cropped mid-thigh or by the hips. A full body shows the character completely, usually for reference sheets or environment-based art. The prices may change as I can create promotions and other limited time events, please check my social media posts to see if I’m running a promo!

Extra costs

If you want a detailed background or environment, more than one character, complicated details and items or variations of the drawing (e.g. different expressions and clothing), there are extra costs which can vary according to each piece. To be fair to the base prices, these extra costs are percentages.

  • Background or environment - Up to +65%
  • Extra character(s) - Up to +50% per character
  • Complicated details or extra items - Up to +40%
  • Variations - Up to +30% per variation
Conditions

Before contacting me, please make sure that your idea and payment methods meet my conditions listed below and that you agree with them.

  • I will only accept PayPal or Stripe as payment methods;
  • I will only accept PayPal or Stripe as payment methods;
  • I will only ask for payment after the client has agreed with the sketch I show;
  • I will only make big changes in the sketch stage, before receiving the payment. After that, I will only make small changes such as lineart details and colors;
  • I CANNOT draw:
    • Furry or anthro characters;
    • Mecha, robots, cars and related (except for, maybe, motorcycles);
    • Real people without consent (characters played by actors and OC faceclaims are ok);
    • NSFW, nudity or fetishes;
    • Anything I’m not comfortable with (feel free to ask!)
